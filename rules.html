Scandal Rules

While you are in this guild you are subject to follow these rules.


Go Home

IN-GAME RULES

1. All Hypixel Rules apply.

2. Please be respectful of all guild members.

3. Keep chat appropriate at all times.

4. Cheating will result in an immediate in-game blacklist from the guild.

5. Refrain from spamming messages in guild chat or guild party chats.

6. Your Discord nick must match your in-game name.

If you are kicked, please understand that the staff team as adequate reasoning to kick you. You are most welcome to reapply and/or ask the staff team why you were kicked.




DISCORD RULES

1. Discrimination or harassment of any form will NOT be tolerated and a punishment will be given accordingly.

2. This is a minecraft guild, there is no need for sexual content.

3. If someone is caught raiding or griefing they will be banned instantly.

4. Spam pinging someone falls under harassment.

5. Do not @mention or direct message Scandal Staff unnecessarily. We have lives too.

6. No spamming the bots, purposefully using glitches to break them, mixing bot channels, so on so forth.

7. No personal information. Protect your privacy and the privacy of others.

8. No harassment, abuse, or bullying. We have zero-tolerance for harming others.

9. No racist, sexist, anti-LGBTQ+, or otherwise offensive content. We have zero- tolerance for hate speech.

10. No political or religious topics. These complex subjects result in controversial and offensive posts.

11. You don't have to do literally everything we say, but you get it.

12. Rules are subject to common sense. These rules are not comprehensive and the use of loopholes to violate the spirit of these rules is subject to enforcement.

13. Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines apply. You must be at least 13 years old to use Discord, and abide by all other terms and guidelines.




NOTICE

The RULES can change at any time for any reason. Make sure you always read the latest rules in our Discord.

If you have any questions regarding the rules, please contact a staff member on our Discord.
